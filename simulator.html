📖 How to Use the Simulator
1. Click Start Practice Call — a random dealership owner appears with a personality and context.
2. Read the scenario and what they said. Say your response out loud — not in your head. This builds muscle memory.
3. Click Reveal Best Response to see the ideal answer and the psychology behind it.
4. Compare your answer to the best response. Note any differences. Click Next Scenario to continue.
5. Aim for 10 reps every day. After 100 total reps, objection handling becomes automatic — you stop thinking and start responding.
